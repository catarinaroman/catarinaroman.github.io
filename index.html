Catarina Roman

I am a PhD Student in Political Science at the University of California, San Diego. I hold a BA in social sciences from the University of São Paulo, in Brazil. My subfields are Comparative Politics and Methodology. I am currently dedicated to political economy projects revolving around compulsory voting laws, informal markets, and political participation. Additionally, I am eager to study how to increase the efficiency of democratic institutions and solve collective action problems, especially in the Latin American context.
Here you will find my latest academic works as well as my future research projects. Hopefully, you may get to know who I am, what I do, and what I am passionate about. Welcome to my page!
