ABOUT

I am Catarina, a 5th-year Political Science Ph.D. candidate at UC San Diego. I am broadly interested in Political Economy and Comparative Politics, with a regional focus on Latin America. My dissertation, provisionally titled Artificial Participation: The Hidden Costs of Compulsory Voting , revolves around the relationship between democratic competition and compulsory voting laws. I am motivated by the challenges democracies face by design, such as low and unequal participation and disproportional representation.
