Catarina Roman

I am a PhD Student in Political Science at the University of California, San Diego. I hold a BA in social sciences from the University of São Paulo, in Brazil.
My subfields are Comparative Politics and Methodology. I am currently dedicated to political economy projects revolving around compulsory voting laws, informal markets, and political participation.
Please scroll down to find my CV, my published articles, working papers, and more information about past and future projects.
